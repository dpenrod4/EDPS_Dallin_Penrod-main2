About Me

I'm a specialist in children's television and understand how to design it in a manner that will bring about positive impact in their behaviors and academic performance.

When studying media arts and film at Brigham Young University I became passionate about children's media upon creating a claymation webseries that teaches kids about mental health. After obtaining a Masters in Human Development and Education from the Harvard Graduate School of Education I worked as a video production specialist for two years before pursuing my PhD in Learning and Cognition at the University of Utah.
